About me

My name is Quinten Wuyts and I'm a 19 year old Game Developer. Currently, I'm a senior Digital Arts and Entertainment student in Howest, Kortrijk (Belgium).
Ever since I had access to a computer, I've been a fond of all sorts of Games. Knowing how these games were created was an interest that came shortly after.
My main interest in the Game Development Pipeline is Programming. I like to figure out mechanics for Games and tweak them until they feel just right.
However, I don't mind helping out in the 3D pipeline where necessary. Thanks to my education, I know how to create 3D assets and VFX Particles.
If you'd like to know more about me, feel free to get in touch with me!
